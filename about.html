About LearnHub

LearnHub is a lightweight, browser-based learning plateform for Web and App development. It's built to help beginners and intermediate learners explore HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python app development with structured courses, lessons, video tutorials, and interactive quizzes. The design prioritizes clarity, dark/lightt mode accessibility, and modular expansion
